What we as POLAECO STAND FOR

We focus on the

celebration of women,

and honour the diversity and freedom of following paths that

allow women to discover their passion.


From women who dedicate themselves to homemaking, bringing value to society by cultivating nurturing environments, as well as representing what true resilience and compassion is, who are the very

backbone behind any thriving community.


To those who choose to step into professional arenas

carrying the strengths of what it is to be woman

into diverse fields from business to the arts.

Polaeco champions this diversity of choice, empowering women to push their personal limits and as such

emerge as leaders and find success in their spaces.


Whether that is from the warmth of home or at the forefront of innovation.

Vision

At Polaeco our vision is a world where young women take the lead, driving positive change. We see a future where mentorship is the cornerstone, connecting a global community of mentors with young women to nurture their leadership potential.
Polaeco bridges generations, uniting the wisdom of influential figures with the aspirations of the next generation. In our vision, gender equality is a lived reality, and young women are not just part of the conversation but leading it.

Purpose

The purpose of Polaeco is to empower and uplift young women, providing them with the skills, courage, and support needed to take on leadership roles in both the public and private sectors. Our mission is to break down barriers and create a future where gender equality is not just an aspiration but a tangible reality.
We envision a world where women are not only participating in leadership but leading with vision, empathy, and inclusivity.
Our commitment is to empower, support, and enable young women to be the architects of a brighter, more equitable tomorrow.




Where does Polaeco come from?

The name POLAECO derives from a play of terms, namely Politics Law and Economics (Po - La - Eco).
Each term representing the key aspects of where our focus is.

Why start Polaeco?

"I have chosen to focus Polaeco on young women as I see them as the most underestimated force in society. A force, that once awakened, can potentially redefine the moral and political compass of nations." - POLAECO CHAIRPERSON

What does Polaeco mean to you?
