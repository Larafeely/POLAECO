Who We Are?

POLAECO is a student-led and women-centred non-profit organization based at the North-West University in Potchefstroom, South Africa.
Our mission is to cultivate and empower future leaders by providing them with business, political, and economic skills through practical education and mentorship.

Our Mission



Empowerment

Provide young women with the courage, knowledge, and skills to assume leadership roles in both public and private sectors.

Mentorship

Foster a global network of mentors who guide, inspire, and support young women's leadership journeys.

Inspiration

Celebrate the voices, dreams, and innovative potential of young women, viewing them as the driving force.

Inclusivity

Ensure that young women from diverse backgrounds have the opportunity to lead, make decisions, and influence change.

Global Impact

Equip young women with the tools and confidence to create positive change within their communities and on a global scale.

What we as POLAECO STAND FOR

We focus on the

celebration of women,

and honour the diversity and freedom of following paths that

allow women to discover their passion.


Polaeco champions this diversity of choice, empowering women to push their personal limits and as such

emerge as leaders and find success in their spaces.

Whether that is the from the warmth of home or the forefront of innovation.
